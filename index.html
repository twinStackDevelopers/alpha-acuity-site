Girls Leadership Conference 2026

Event Completed 250+ Attendees

A Historic Success!

The 2026 Girls Leadership Conference was our most impactful yet, bringing together over 250 young women from across Zambia for a transformative day of leadership development. Attendees participated in workshops led by 12 industry leaders, heard from 5 inspiring keynote speakers, and engaged in mentorship circles that have continued beyond the event. The conference concluded with the inaugural "Future Women Leaders Pledge" ceremony, where all participants committed to driving change in their communities.
